March 9th 2018

Added profit margin calculations to the humble bundle store section. It assumes you are selling to dedicated buyers and DOES NOT include listing, payout and currency conversion fees since those are not on a per item basis. As always, please double check the g2a link and humble bundle link to make sure they are for the same game. The initial sort of the humble bundle sales are now based on the profit margin percentages, though you can still use the sorting features from before. Site is updated at 22:00 GMT daily, so data may not be accurate during this time. Something similar is coming for the steam sales.

Margin = (G2A_Price * 0.892 - 0.43) - Humble_Store_Price

Margin_Percent = G2A_Price / Humble_Store_Price * 100


Note: 0.35 EUR = 0.43 USD at the moment. Calculations are done with static conversion rates, but can be made to use dynamic rates if requested.
